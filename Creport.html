CYBER-ELITE

The flagship initiative CYBER ELITE ,organized by IETE-ISF VBIT, was conducted with the objective of creating cybersecurity awareness among students by equipping them with essential knowledge and practical skills to protect their digital identity. The program focused on introducing fundamental cybersecurity concepts and enhancing student preparedness through interactive sessions, hands-on activities, and real-time simulations, thereby strengthening their ability to navigate the digital world securely and responsibly.
