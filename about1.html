About Us

Sowparnika, the flagship event of IETE-ISF VBIT, embarked on its journey a decade ago with a distinct focus on fostering innovation and creativity among first-year students. This event serves as a platform for these budding minds to showcase their ideas and talents through a variety of engaging activities, including working models, poster presentations, and quizzes. The primary objective is to encourage active participation and skill development as part of the outcome-based education approach.
