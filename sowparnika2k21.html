Sowparnika, the flagship event of VBIT, started a decade ago especially conducted for the first years, encourages to showcase their ideas by engaging in various activities like working models, poster presentations, quiz, and PowerPoint presentations. It assists the first-year students as a part of outcome-based education.

❖The event has begun with greeting our Chairman, Principal, Vice Principal, Head of the Departments, and few Senior Faculties in the Departments, faculty coordinators of Sowparnika and department of H&S.
❖In the beginning Vice Principal, S.L.N. Sridhar sir addressed the session followed by Kulkarni sir, about the significance of Sowparnika for the first-year students.
❖The faculty of the H&S Department was divided into three panels for the evaluation of PPT and Poster presentation i.e. 2 panels for PPT presentation and 1 panel for Poster presentation.
❖Each panel is provided with two student coordinators of IETE and 3 volunteers.
❖The Evaluators have been provided an evaluation sheet for evaluating the participant’s performance based on their presentation.
❖The session has begun, participants started giving their presentations which is based on technical topics and answered the questions of the evaluators, for which they are marked.
❖After the completion of presentations in all panels, all the students joined in the same session.
❖ The stage of Sowparnika was open for the students; they shared their outlooks on the event.
❖The Evaluators also shared their views on the event and guided the students for their betterment.
❖Then the event has ended with a vote of thanks
