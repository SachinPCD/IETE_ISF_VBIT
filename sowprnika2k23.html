Sowparnika, the flagship event of VBIT, started a decade ago especially conducted for the first years, encourages to show case their ideas by engaging in various activities like working models, poster presentations, quiz, and PowerPoint presentations. It assists the first-year students as a part of outcome-based education. For the last three years, SOWPARNIKA is being organized by IETE-ISF VBIT in collaboration with the department of physics and chemistry. This unique event always has a special importance in our college especially for 1st year students.
